WHO WE ARE

Revolutionizing How India Hires
Daily Wage & Skilled Labor

Founded in 2020, 1Day Labour was born out of a simple yet powerful motive: to bridge the gap between temporary workers seeking daily employment and clients in need of immediate, reliable manpower.

We noticed that the traditional labor hiring process was deeply fragmented. Customers faced endless bargaining, unreliability, and lack of safety, while hard-working daily wage earners struggled to find consistent employment. Our mission is to digitize this workspace, ensuring instant booking, standard transparent pricing, and Aadhaar-verified safety.

Our Core Motive

To eliminate daily uncertainty for workers and provide instant on-demand labor to households and businesses within minutes.
